Hadith Studies (علم الحديث)

Master the second major source of Islam—the Prophetic Traditions.

Level: Ultra-Advanced Duration: 6+ Months Frequency: 3 sessions per week

Preserving the Heritage

Hadith studies (Uloom al-Hadith) focus on the methodology behind preserving, classifying, and authenticating the sayings and actions of the Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ). This course is ideal for students who want a deep scholarly understanding of Islamic law and history.

  • Mustalah al-Hadith (Terminology & Methodology)
  • Study of Major Hadith Collections (Bukhari, Muslim)
  • Authentication and Classification (Sahih, Hasan, Da'if)
  • Application in Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence)
  • Memorization of core 40 Hadiths (Imam Nawawi)
  • Chains of Transmission Analysis (Asma ur Rijal)
