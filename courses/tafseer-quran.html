Uncover the wisdom and depths of the Divine Word through scholarly insight.

Course Overview: Connecting with Wisdom

The Quran is not just a book to be recited; it is a guide for life. Tafseer-ul-Quran is the science of understanding and interpreting the verses revealed to Prophet Muhammad (ï·º). At Usman Ibne Affan Online Quran Academy, we believe every Muslim should have the opportunity to know what Allah is asking of them. This course aims to bridge the gap between simple translation and deep intellectual and spiritual understanding.

Our scholars guide you through the classical and modern commentaries (Tafseer) to help you grasp the historical context (Shan-e-Nuzool), linguistic nuances, and practical applications of each verse. This is an essential journey for anyone looking to increase their concentration (Khushu) in prayer and their love for the Divine.

Primary Learning Outcomes

By enrolling in our Tafseer course, you will achieve the following:

  • Thematic Understanding: Analyzing the core themes of different Surahs, such as Oneness of Allah (Tawheed), Prophethood (Risalat), and the Afterlife (Akhirah).
  • Historical Context (Shan-e-Nuzool): Learning the events and circumstances surrounding the revelation of specific verses to understand their true application.
  • Linguistic Insight: Exploring the miraculous nature of Quranic Arabic and why specific words were chosen by Allah.
  • Jurisprudential Rulings: Understanding the verses that establish Islamic law (Ahkam) and how they apply in modern life.
  • Spiritual Application: Translating Quranic wisdom into personal character building and improved worship.

Our Teaching Methodology

We use a research-based and interactive methodology:

Scholarly Comparison: We reference authentic classical works like Tafseer Ibn Kathir and Tafseer al-Jalalayn, while also discussing modern insights that help relate the Quran to 21st-century challenges.

Discussion and Q&A: Unlike a passive lecture, our classes are interactive. Students are encouraged to ask questions, share reflections, and discuss how the verses apply to their daily lives.

Visual Mind Mapping: Our teachers often use mind maps to connect verses and themes, helping students visualize the cohesive structure of the Quran.

Focus on the Soul: We emphasize 'Tazkiyah' (self-purification), ensuring that the knowledge gained leads to a positive change in the student's heart and manners.
