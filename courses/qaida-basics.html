The essential foundation course for beginner students worldwide to learn Quran with Tajweed.

Course Overview: Building the Foundation

The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. For the student of the Quran, that first step is mastering the Noorani Qaida. This foundational course is specifically designed for children and adults who are beginning their path toward Quranic literacy. Whether you are a non-native speaker or a young child experiencing the Arabic script for the first time, our curriculum provides the structural support needed to transition from simple letter recognition to fluid reading.

At Usman Ibne Affan Online Quran Academy, we understand that the quality of your foundation determines the beauty of your recitation. A weak foundation in alphabets or pronunciation can lead to lifelong habits that are difficult to correct later. That is why our instructors prioritize patience and precision above all else. We utilize globally recognized methods like the Noorani Qaida and Madani Qaida, which have been proven over decades to simplify complex phonetic rules into manageable lessons.

Primary Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, our students are expected to achieve complete independence in identifying and pronouncing Arabic text. The curriculum is divided into modules that focus on:

  • Alphabet Mastery: Deep recognition of all 29 Arabic letters (Huroof-e-Tahajji) in their isolated, beginning, middle, and end forms.
  • Phonetic Precision (Makharij): Learning exactly where each sound originates in the throat and mouth to ensure distinct pronunciation.
  • Harakaat (Vowels): Understanding the short vowels (Fatha, Kasra, Damma) and how they change the sound of a letter instantly.
  • Tanween & Sukoon: Mastering the rules of double vowels and the silent state of letters, which are crucial for rhythmic reading.
  • Maddah Rules: Learning the elongation rules that give Quranic recitation its unique, melodic quality.
  • Independent Reading: Graduating from single letters to compound words (Murakkabat) and eventually full verses with confidence.

Our Teaching Methodology

We believe that every student learns differently. Our One-on-One Virtual Classroom environment allows teachers to tailor the pace to the student's individual capability.

Interactive Visual Aids: Our teachers use screen-sharing and digital whiteboards to highlight specific letters and rules, making the abstract concepts of phonetics visual and tangible.

Repetition and Reinforcement: Every lesson begins with a review of the previous day's work. We believe that 'Sabqi' (today's lesson) is only successful if the 'Sabq' (past lessons) is deeply rooted in the heart.

Parental Involvement for Kids: For our younger students, we provide regular progress reports to parents, detailing which rules have been mastered and which areas need more practice. This collaborative effort ensures that the child feels supported both during and after their online session.

Female Tutors for Comfort: We offer dedicated female tutors for sisters and young children, ensuring a comfortable and respectful learning environment that adheres to Islamic values of privacy and modesty.
