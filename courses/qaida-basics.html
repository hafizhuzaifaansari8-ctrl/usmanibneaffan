Qaida for Beginners

The essential foundation for reading the Holy Quran with Tajweed.

Level: Beginner Duration: 2-3 Months Ages: 4+ and Adults

Course Overview

This course is designed for those who are just starting their Quranic journey. Whether you are a child or an adult, our expert teachers will guide you through the Arabic alphabet, vowels, and basic joining rules using the world-renowned Noorani Qaida or Madani Qaida.

What You Will Learn

  • Recognition of Arabic Letters (Huroof-e-Tahajji)
  • Correct Pronunciation (Makharij ur Huroof)
  • Vowels (Haroof-e-Maddah & Lin)
  • Rules of Tanween and Noon Sakinah
  • Basic joining of letters (Murakkabat)
  • Rules of Stopping (Waqf)
