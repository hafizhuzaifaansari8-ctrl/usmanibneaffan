The Key to Understanding

Arabic grammar is the foundation of all Islamic sciences. Our intensive course covers both Nahw (Syntax) and Sarf (Morphology) specifically as they apply to the Quranic text, allowing students to understand why verbs and nouns take their specific forms.

  • Ilm al-Sarf (Morphology & Word derivation)
  • Ilm al-Nahw (Sentence construction & I'rab)
  • Analysis of Quranic verses (Tarkeeb)
  • Mastery of Verb Conjugations (Abwaab)
  • Advanced Noun Categories (Ism al-Fa'il, Ism al-Maf'ool)
  • Practical Exercises from the Holy Quran
